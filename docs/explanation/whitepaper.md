# Whitepaper

The DIZZY whitepaper is the philosophy-and-motivation document: why software architecture
so often fails, the principles a good architecture must satisfy, and how DIZZY's
feature-file approach answers them.

It is authored and maintained in [Typst](https://typst.app/) and compiled to PDF.

- **Read it:** [whitepaper.pdf](../whitepaper.pdf)
- **Build it:** `just whitepaper` (compiles `docs/whitepaper.typ`)

!!! note
    The whitepaper and its supporting sources (`figures.typ`, `glossary.typ`, `refs.bib`,
    and the `pnf/` mini-essays) are **maintainer-authored** and live at the `docs/` root,
    outside this generated site.
